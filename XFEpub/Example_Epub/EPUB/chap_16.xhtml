Chapter 15

Junichi was waiting for me near this big banyan tree we have in the center of the base when I went to talk to him the next morning.

"The snow woman," he said when I approached him "he said something had its eyes on you."

"Yeah, I had a run with what might be a kitsune not long after I came here. I see it from time to time."

"A run in?"

"It was staring at me, I went out to shoo it away, got my face messed up."

"And it stalks you?"

"I catch glimpses of it from time to time but it hasn't gotten too close."

"How many tails?"

"Two. I think." Junichi gave a quiet 'hmm.'

"So if it's got two tails, what am I in for?"

"It is nowhere near as powerful as it could be, but you would still trifle with it at your own risk."

"Would shooting at it work?" I asked. I felt like this was a pretty dumb question but I still wanted to get an actual answer. Junichi just stared at me.

"I would just run."

Just then, we saw Webber walking by with a clipboard in his hand. I snapped to attention as he got closer.

"Mornin' sir."

"Thank you, Private Lang."

"Permission to ask a question, sir?" Webber sighed.

"Let me guess…" I gulped.

"It's about… you know what, sir."

"Fine. As long as it isn't too intrusive."

"Oof, um, ok." Webber walked closer to me.

"Just spit it out, please." I gulped.

"When do you think you'll tell your family about this?"

"Never," he replied. "If they find out, it won't be from me." I blinked. Junichi did a double take.

"S-sir, don't they have-"

"No private, they don't have the right to know. What I went through when I lived with my parents tells me that nothing good's going to come from letting them know they have a granddaughter."

I was just… stunned. Junichi looked like he could barely comprehend what he was hearing.

"Sir, I won't pry but I'm starting to wonder what you'd have lived through to decide not to tell them."

"Good. It means your parents took their responsibilities seriously. I'll put it this way. Hanabi might have been unexpected, but I promised myself that if I had any kids I'd keep them away from my parents. Nothing good's going to come of Hanabi meeting them. Same for Aiko."

He walked away after that. I was constantly replaying his words in my head.

What the fuck did he go through that would make him decide he couldn't trust his parents to behave around his kid?
