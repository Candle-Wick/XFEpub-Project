Armory

Special:
1. Bionics - BP used is customized in each, Minimum 5 and Maximum of 50 BP. This will determine sophistication and articulation. The Master of the Forge can make an attempt to make a truly customized product through a roll though it is not necessary. Bionic Replacements at this time must be specified due to the limited number of Tech-Marines available and limited production facilities. This isn't the Salamanders or Iron Hands which have materials set aside for such things in abundance.
2. Relics/Master Crafted/Etc - BP is used to have Martellus or a truly proficient Senior Techmarine attempt to create a truly astonishing weapon, armor, vehicle, or device. The minimum and maximum of constructing personal gear is x2.5 the BP cost of the item, due to the time and effort involved with creating something truly amazing off of template. During the creation, rolls will be made to see if the item created is exceptional, slightly better, or in fact may end up as a dud entirely. Some things, like Land Raiders, are already flat out exceptional creations and cannot be created in this manner.
