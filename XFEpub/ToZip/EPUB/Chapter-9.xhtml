The Sanctorium Arcanum
A secret cavernous hemispherical chamber within the battle barge Litany of Fury which contains a powerful choir of priests, mystics and astropaths. These individuals encircle a central altar that is held by a single beam of silver light which pierces from the sky doom at the apex of the massive curving ceiling. Above the altar piece is the Beacon Psykana which is tended to by the Librarians of the Secret Order of Psykana. The chambers of the Sanctorium are considered unique but pale before the majesty of the Chamber of the Astronomican on Terra. One of the benefits the Blood Ravens possess is that the Sanctorium Arcanum is a deeply held secret which has thus far prevented their enemies from assaulting it. The Sanctorium is heavily defended, capable of withstanding assault from both conventional as well as psychic means. It is also a traditional for the remains of a lost Librarian of the Secret Order of Psykana to be placed at the altar of the Sanctorium Arcanum for a hundred days. A special psychically shielded room within these chambers also holds the Psykana Armorium.

- Here, the remains of an Avatar of Khaine summoned by the forces of Biel-Tan under the Farseer Macha, defeated on the world of Tartarus, are stored for study and safekeeping.
- Here, the remains of Craftworld Ulthwé's Avatar of Khaine, slain on the planet Kronus during the Dark Crusade, are stored for study and safekeeping.
- Here, the remains of an Avatar of Khaine summoned by the Craftworld Ulthwé, slain by Aramus within the Typhon Arena before the planet's destruction later on, are stored for study and safekeeping.
- Here, the remains of the Unknown Craftworld buried on Typhon Primaris' Avatar of Khaine, slain by Aramus during the Aurelian Crusades, are stored for study and safekeeping.
- Here, the remains of the Seer Council of Craftworld Biel-Tan, from weapon, armor, and talismans are stored for study and safekeeping.

The Psykana Armorium
Deep within the Litany of Fury, located within a specially shielded chamber inside the Sanctorium Arcanum. A secret armory containing only force weapons, many of which have been procured through the ages by the Blood Raven Librarians during encounters with the Eldar. Whilst some were through victory in combat, it is whispered that some of these weapons were given voluntarily by the Eldar. The existence of the Psykana Armorium is a closely kept secret which is only revealed to initiates of the Secret Order of Psykana. Whist it is called an armory, it is not compared to normal ones as it serves as a repository of special weapons as well as serves as a conduit for psychic potentials of the weapons it held. These are channeled through the Beacon Psykana itself and amplified its psychic signal that pulses in the hearts of the Blood Ravens within the Order which it is maintained by. Whenever new Librarians are inducted into the Order Psykana, they are free to choose a weapon from the Psykana Armorium as their own.
