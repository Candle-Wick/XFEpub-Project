Inch By Bloody Inch
+020.M42+ To +025.M42+
+A Foe Without Honour Is A Foe Already Beaten.+
​
It has been a hard fought five years against the daemons of the warp. But it is undeniable that progress has been made. With each specialized Gellar field generator, the Judgement of Carrion twists and shudders as it is forced into reality further and further. The immaterium which has drenched it for so long is being steadily forced back. Vast forge ships from Zerann and Aerus float about its great bulk, each massive vessel already practically the size of a regular battle barge on its own yet with far more of them unequipped with weaponry so much as their incredibly sophisticated internal forge systems. With the coldest precision and most unforgiving of logic do the servants of the Omnissiah tear away those parts of the space hulk which are of clear xeno-nature while smoothing out the lines and partitions between its various component parts. Ever so slowly, as more of the warp is pushed back, are they transforming the unpredictably shifting space hulk instead into something more worthy of their God.

But the fight is being won, if with extreme work. After so much constant exertion of their skitarii and own military power within the past few years, it is unlikely that the Mechanicus within Korianis will be capable of committing to much more for some time. Operations as Aerus or this will be rare for a time. Part of you is thankful, at the thought that in the future the Blood Ravens might be able to move forward and fight other foes beyond just Korianis itself. Perhaps. But such thoughts are not uncommon on the Carrion, for its blood-soaked and rusted corridors are morose in turn. There are brighter moments of course, that the 10th​ Company has returned, bloodied and successful with new battle brothers to join your ranks. Even as you battle within, that does not prevent you from leading the Chapter itself. Indeed, there is regular and required times for all to be removed from the space hulk lest it bring taint into your bodies, minds, and souls. It is something that even the Mechanicus knows for true, given the amount of their own servitors and skitarii which mutate and turn against them if given too much long-term exposure. Hadrian has recovered from his wounds, and the rest of the Chapter remains to be lead for the future.
